The two fixed points

The festivals

These are the anchors. Everything else fits around them.

01first
Dress code: white

PDL White Ocean — Festa Branca

Ponta Delgada turns out head-to-toe in white for the city's biggest open-air night — 20 stages through the historic centre, music till 4am, the whole town on its feet.

Likely Sat Aug 1 · 7:30pm–4am · Ponta Delgada Open city centre in Maps ↗
02second
Beachside main stage

MEO Monte Verde Festival

The Azores' biggest music festival, on the sand at Praia do Monte Verde — 10 min from our base. 2026 line-up includes Two Door Cinema Club, James, Calema and Xutos & Pontapés.

Aug 6–8, 2026 · Praia do Monte Verde, Ribeira Grande Open venue in Maps ↗

Heads up on dates: White Ocean is a single Saturday night and usually falls on the first Saturday of August — Aug 1 in 2026 — but the city hasn't published the official 2026 date yet. That puts the two events about 5 days apart, so the days in between (roughly Aug 2–5) are our flexible window for everything below.
